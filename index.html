About

We created Pet Pointers to help ease the experience of pet ownership and make sure every animal can get their forever home

Keeping the Pets in Mind


The goal with this app is to help families and individuals learn the responsibilities of pet care in a controlled environment. Many people don't realize just how much work and time it takes to take of a pet, especially shelter animals. The experience of Pet Pointers is grounded in the idea of making sure that pets are no longer sent back to the shelter, and this is done though building stronger connections with the user.


User Centered Design


Month of research went into this project to make it fun and easy to use. Many groups were researched including; potential pet owners, current pet owners, trainers, vets, and shelters. All of this research was done to make the experience easy and effective. Every feature and page in this app is designed to make sure that you know what it takes to own a pet and what steps to take next. Through our Pet Points you will know exactly how to take care of your new friend when your bring them to their new home!



Building Lasting Connections


Having a pet is an extremely rewarding experience and we want you to have all the fun and love that a pet can have with less of the stress so that you can focus on what really matters, making a lasting bond. Shelter animals have been through a lot and they need you to be there for them because you are the key to their forever home. Although these animals come with baggage once you can make a connection they will be your best friend for the rest of their lives.

FAQ

Through all of the research done and animal care professionals consulted we have put together the best way you can keep up the habits that will lead to responsible pet ownership.

Adopting a pet comes with a lot of responibility, both financialy and emotionaly. We help you by showing how much time and money it really takes to properly care for an animal companion (even the surprise stuff). If you decide to adopt a pet without all this knowledge you may get in over your head faster than you could say "ruff".

There are a lot of different resources for different pets that all have different information. This leads to confusion and frustration when your are trying to figure out something that ends up being simple, but you still put hours of work into the research. Pet pointers puts all the resources for that specific pet in one place (and it happens to be in your pocket!). Now there is no more confusion and all the answers you could need are in your hand, and we know just where to find it!
